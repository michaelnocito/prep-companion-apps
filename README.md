# Excel Sales Sprint

**Help Srbina go from "I know a little Excel" to interview-ready confidence — one spreadsheet round at a time.**

A free, silly, browser-based game where you play through short Excel exercises designed for people starting out in data analytics. No install. No account. Just open it and play.

→ **[Play it live](https://michaelnocito.github.io/excel-sales-sprint/)**

---

## What it teaches

- Reading a table without panicking
- Writing `SUM` and `AVERAGE` formulas
- Connecting a number to a plain-English business takeaway
- Sorting data and identifying standout performers

Each round layers on the one before it. You learn through repetition, not flashcards.

---

## How it works

- Open the link above in any browser
- Read the mini spreadsheet
- Answer the prompt by typing a formula, a number, or a short explanation
- Get instant feedback, earn achievements, and keep your streak going
- Sounds, dark mode, and a restart button are all included

---

## Beta — feedback genuinely welcome

This is a beta. It works, it's playable, and it's already teaching things — but there's more to build.

If you play it and something feels off, too hard, too easy, unclear, or missing — **open an issue or drop a comment.** I'm building this in the open and I'd love to develop it alongside people who are actually learning.

**Want to contribute?** PRs are open. Ideas are welcome. If you're learning Excel yourself, you're exactly the right person to help improve this.
